### Astronomer
[Common Class]
Uses orbiting objects to fend off foes
- +2 Scythes
- +2 Shields
- -25% Damage

### Domain Extension
[Common]
Increases the size of orbitals and their orbits
- +1 Orbital size
- -25% Scythe damage

### Faster Shields
[Common]
Increases the speed of shields
- +50% Shield speed
- +0.25s Block cooldown

### Gravity Well
[Uncommon]
Increases the speed of orbitals
- +25% Speed
- +100% Orbital speed
- -25% Health

- -25% Damage

[Common]

[Common]

[Uncommon Subclass]
*Requires Dual Shields and Twin Scythes*
Stalwart Defenders. Their shields defend them from all bullets
- +4 Shields
- +50% Health
- +4 Shield size
- -4 Scythes

### Perfect Guard
[Rare]
*Requires Guardian*
An inpenetrable shield of light
- +8 Shields

### Harvester
[Uncommon Subclass]
*Requires Dual Shields and Twin Scythes*
Reapers of souls. Defense is not their concern. Their scythes don't cut through bullets
- +4 Scythes

- -4 Shields

### Dark Harvest
[Rare]

- +50% Scythe damage
